FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is Azeel Studio really free?

Yes. The core editor, templates and exports are free forever. We may add optional pro features later.

Do my files leave my device?

No. Editing happens 100% in your browser. Autosave uses local storage on your device.

Can I install it as an app?

Yes. Azeel Studio is a PWA — install it from your browser menu for offline use.

What formats can I export?

PNG, JPG and WEBP at 1×, 2× or custom scale, with adjustable quality.

Does it work on mobile?

Absolutely. The editor is touch-first with pinch-zoom, drag and gesture controls.
